In How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days, Andie Anderson, a writer for Composure magazine, embarks on a mission to drive a man away within ten days for an article titled "How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days." Simultaneously, advertising executive Benjamin Barry accepts a bet to make a woman fall in love with him in the same timeframe, leading to a comedic clash of intentions as their paths cross.
