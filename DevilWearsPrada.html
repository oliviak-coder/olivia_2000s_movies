    The Devil Wears Prada (2006) follows Andrea Sachs (Anne Hathaway), an aspiring journalist who lands a job as the assistant to Miranda Priestly (Meryl Streep), the demanding editor-in-chief of Runway magazine. As Andrea navigates the high-pressure fashion industry, she faces personal and professional challenges that test her values and ambitions.
